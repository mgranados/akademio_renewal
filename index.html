How to apply Traction to your startup?

  • Brainstorming

    Pitch at least one idea for all channels!

  • Ranking

    Place your ideas for each channel in one of 3 columns, Inner Circle(best), Potential(good) and Long Shot(not so good).

  • Prioritize

    Select JUST 3 channels that seems the most promising.

  • Testing

    Micro-test those 3 channels and select the one that works best for you.

  • Focus

    Concentrate all your efforts on that one channel!
